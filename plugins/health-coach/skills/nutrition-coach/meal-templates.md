# Meal templates and daily log format

## Daily log file template

When creating a new day's log, use this structure (save as `data/<user>/YYYY-MM-DD.md`):

```markdown
# <Day of week>, YYYY-MM-DD — <user>

## Meals

### Breakfast
- Time:
- What:
- Rough macros: P __ g / C __ g / F __ g / ~__ kcal
- Notes:

### Lunch
- Time:
- What:
- Rough macros:
- Notes:

### Snack
- Time:
- What:

### Dinner
- Time:
- What:
- Rough macros:
- Notes:

## Water
- ~__ L

## Energy / mood
-

## Daily totals (estimated)
- Protein: __ g
- Carbs: __ g
- Fat: __ g
- Calories: ~__ kcal
- Fiber: ~__ g

## Note for tomorrow
-
```

## Go-to balanced meals

Quick reference for meal planning suggestions. All are ~30-45 min max and hit the plate model reasonably well.

### Breakfasts

1. **Greek yogurt + oats + fruit + nuts** — 5 min. ~400 kcal, ~20 g protein.
2. **Whole grain toast + avocado + 2 scrambled eggs + tomato** — 10 min. ~450 kcal, ~22 g protein.
3. **Cottage cheese bowl + honey + berries + chia seeds** — 5 min. ~350 kcal, ~25 g protein.

### Lunches

4. **Chickpea salad with tuna, hard-boiled egg, tomato, cucumber and olive oil** — 10 min (canned chickpeas). ~500 kcal, ~30 g protein.
5. **Stir-fried brown rice + chicken + mixed vegetables + soy sauce** — 25 min (with pre-cooked rice, 15 min). ~600 kcal, ~35 g protein.
6. **Lentils with vegetables and hard-boiled egg on top** — 30 min. ~500 kcal, ~25 g protein.

### Dinners

7. **Baked salmon + roasted potato + asparagus** — 30 min. ~550 kcal, ~35 g protein.
8. **Omelet with 3 eggs + large salad + whole grain bread** — 10 min. ~450 kcal, ~22 g protein.
9. **Vegetable soup + grilled chicken breast + bread** — 25 min. ~450 kcal, ~35 g protein.
10. **Stir-fried tofu with broccoli and rice noodles** — 20 min. ~500 kcal, ~25 g protein.

### Snacks

11. **Apple + handful of almonds** — ~200 kcal, 6 g protein.
12. **Greek yogurt + cinnamon** — ~150 kcal, 15 g protein.
13. **Hummus + crudités (carrot, celery, bell pepper)** — ~200 kcal, 7 g protein.
