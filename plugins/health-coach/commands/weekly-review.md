Produce a weekly review for the user. Follow this sequence:

1. Read all daily log files from `data/` for the last 7 days (filenames follow `YYYY-MM-DD.md`). If some days are missing, note that — don't invent data.
2. For each domain, surface ONE pattern (not a full report):
   - **Sleep**: average duration, consistency of bedtime/wake time, any notable bad or good nights.
   - **Nutrition**: consistent wins (protein, veg, water), and one area to tweak.
   - **Activity**: total sessions/time, intensity distribution, whether load looks sustainable.
3. Then, look for **cross-domain connections** — this is the real value of having all three tracked together. Examples:
   - Poor sleep on days after hard evening training?
   - Lower appetite / skipped meals on high-stress or poorly-slept days?
   - Better workouts after nights with >7h sleep?
4. End with **one** suggestion for next week. Just one.

Total response should fit comfortably in one screen. No bulleted walls of text. If you have nothing useful to say in a category, say so and move on.
